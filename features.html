Platform Features

Everything in one platform

15+ features spanning AI predictions, real-time dashboards, simulated trading, expert portfolios, and community forums — tailored by user role.

Core Trading Features
Real-Time Stock Dashboard
Live stock prices, trends, candlestick charts, trading volumes, and market data. Search any of the 10 tracked NASDAQ tech stocks and monitor price movements instantly.
Basic + Premium
AI Stock Prediction
XGBoost + FinBERT hybrid model predicts next-day stock movement using technical indicators and financial news sentiment. Data range: 2021–2026 across 10 large-cap NASDAQ stocks.
Basic (limited) · Premium (unlimited)
Watchlist Management
Add, view, monitor, and remove stocks from a personal watchlist. Real-time price updates and status checks keep your shortlisted stocks always in view.
Basic + Premium
Simulated Portfolio
Buy and sell stocks with virtual funds — no real money required. Practice investment strategies, track performance, and review your full transaction history.
Basic + Premium
Stock Recommendations
Buy, hold, or sell suggestions generated from AI prediction results and the user's personal risk profile. Recommendations are filtered and personalized per user.
Basic + Premium
Risk Assessment Profiling
The system identifies each user's risk tolerance level to personalise stock recommendations and investment guidance — ensuring advice matches the user's financial comfort zone.
Basic + Premium
